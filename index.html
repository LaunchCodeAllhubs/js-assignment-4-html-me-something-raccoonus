Zacks Favorite Video Games

Today, I will be showing you my favorite video games throughout my childhood. These games helped me through tough times, made great memories, and made me shed a few tears for the emotionally draining games.

The Sly Cooper Series

This is one of the earlier memories I had playing a game made by the company Naughty Dog. It was one of the first story games that really had me hooked since Sly Cooper and the Thievious Raccoonus which was the first game. The 3rd game was the BEST one and I will debate anyone on that!

Jak and Daxter

Jak and Daxter, just like the game before, was developed by Naughty Dog and once again hit a homerun here. You play as a boy who has a friend that gets turned into an ottsel (which is a hybird of an otter and weasel). Your quest is to stop the evil villains Gol and Maia from flooding the world with a substance called Dark Eco. Again, the 3rd game has a different plot than the first, it's much more mature using weapons and vehicles, but the first one is where my love for the game started.
